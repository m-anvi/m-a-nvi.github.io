reflection 7/10

To make my website accessible to as many users as possible, I am (planning to) include alternative text descriptions for all of my images. I will also reduce the amount of links users have to follow to get to different parts of the page, so that people with limited mobility have a simpler time navigating my website. People have been doing some small things to their code to change the design and overall look of their page in a really awesome way. I have seen a lot of creative uses of fonts, text colors, background colors, and text allignments which I will look into incorporating with my own website. This is the first time I am working with HTML from scratch. I usually just edit websites that already have a basic framework. I feel proud that I am finally learning how to create something entirely new.
